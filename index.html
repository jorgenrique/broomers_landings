Who am I?

I’m an out of the box thinker. I believe everyone has the power to change the world (each one in it’s own way), that rules are made to be broken and that bars can be bended. I’m edgy, outgoing, detail oriented and like to work in a friendly environment. That’s why I’m always looking forward to new challenges, opportunities to grow and getting out of my comfort zone.

I’m passionate about product design and improvement, listening and analyzing input data, and coming up with innovative solutions based on the data received. If you feel we could work together, please don’t hesitate to contact me, through any of my social networks or email! Thanks!
